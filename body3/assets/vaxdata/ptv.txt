Pentavalent Vaccine

About-
Pentavalent vaccine is a combined vaccine to protect children from five diseases Diptheria, Tetanus, Pertusis, Haemophilis influenza type b infection and Hepatitis B.
When to give - 
Three doses are given at 6, 10 and 14 weeks of age (can be given till one year of age).
Route and site-
Pentavalent vaccine is given intramuscularly on anterolateral side of mid thigh
