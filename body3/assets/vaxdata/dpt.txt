DPT booster

About-
DPT is a combined vaccine; it protects children from Diphtheria, Tetanus and Pertussis.
When to give -
DPT vaccine is given at 16-24 months of age is called as DPT first booster and DPT 2nd booster is given at 5-6 years of age.
Route and site- 
DPT first booster is given as intramuscular injection in antero-lateral side of mid-thigh in left leg. DPT second booster is given as intramuscular injection in left upper arm.
