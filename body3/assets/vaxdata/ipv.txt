fIPV

About- 
fIPV stands for Fractional Inactivated Poliomylitis Vaccine. It is used to boost the protection against poliomylitis.
When to give- 
Two fractional doses of IVP are given intradermally at 6 and 14 weeks of age.
Route and site- 
It is given as intradermal injection at right upper arm.
